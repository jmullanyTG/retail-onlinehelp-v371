Rate of Sale Calculator – Assortment Planning ROS Scenario Detail

Review the actual items that were used in the calculation of each performance band in the Scenario Results section and selectively exclude items from the calculation

  1. Undock the Scenario Detail Widget from the right hand side of the screen
  1. Click on the Scenario Detail button and then right click the heading and select “float”

  1. The Scenario Detail widget can now be moved around and resized to user preference
  2. Review Scenario Details and exclude items from the scenario
  1. The scenario detail displays all items that were used in the calculation and where they fall in the performance range
  2. To exclude an item from the calculation uncheck the item and hit Save

  1. Once saved, the scenario must be recalculated by clicking on the Calculate button at the top of the screen

  1. Once calculated the scenario results will display the results of the scenario without the excluded items
