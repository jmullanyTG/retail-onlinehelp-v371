The third component is where the user applies the results of a Rate of Sale scenario.  Users select a product filter to identify potential placeholders/variants to apply the results to. Users have the ability to apply the ROS results to multiple placeholders/variants and/or collections directly in this tool. The application of ROS in this view is integrated with the assortment planning module, so once the user has applied the results, the demand plan for the items selected will be adjusted to reflect the ROS values that have been applied.

  1. Select a product filter containing the placeholders/variants that are eligible for ROS application.
  2. Select the time range during which the desired placeholders/variants are in phase in the assortment plan set for the scenario
  3. Select the level at which to apply the ROS: placeholder collection or placeholder

  1. Click Execute to bring in the item results for the criteria that has been selected above
  2. In the Scenario results view, check the box of the ROS to apply to the placeholder/variant/cluster combinations

  1. Check the grand total box of the variants/placeholders or collections in which to make them eligible for the ROS values selected in the scenario results view

  1. Click Copy Rate of Sale at the top of the bottom view to populate the selected ROS values from the results section
  1. Click Save to finish the exercise of utilizing the ROS calculator to impact the demand plans within assortment planning.

Note: Before clicking apply, users can clear any copied ROS values by clicking on the Undo Icon in the top of the apply rate of sale view.
