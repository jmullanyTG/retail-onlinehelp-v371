Rate of Sale Calculator Overview

The Rate of Sale Calculator can be used to model scenarios to help a user determine how much, in units or revenue, a particular placeholder/collection or variant has the potential to sell per site per week.  Often, merchandise planners and allocators are required to make assumptions around the potential volume an item can deliver before committing to materials and raising a purchase order or executing an allocation within the business.  This tool is designed to assist those roles within an organization to make it easier and more efficient to determine a reasonable rate of sale in a site cluster, site or site group, given the historical sales of similar items, as defined by the user. These rates of sale can, then, be applied to demand plans, whether in assortment plans or allocation strategies.  This tool is integrated with both the JustEnough Assortment Planning module and the Allocation module to support the application of demand for merchandise planning and inventory execution within an organization.
