Plan Approvals

As part of the 3.6.0 release JustEnough has introduced a more robust standard plan approval process into the financial planning module.  This means that our clients have the ability to review, approve, reject and merge branch plans as needed.  These plan statuses can support pre-season and in-season plan reviews.  The process of reviewing, approving, rejecting and merging plans is contained within the Financial Plan Maintenance view.
