Lock Controls

  • After engaging a lock, the system will conditionally format the locked cells for easy identification. Aggregate level locks are a slightly darker color than low level locks

  • Note: valid only for sales related metrics
  • : unlocks the selected cell and dependent cells, making it editable and able to be changed based on changes made elsewhere in the view (see Lock & Spread below for more information)
  • Note: valid only for sales related metrics
  • : removes all locks from the current view, including those listed in the aggregate lock pane, regardless of whether they are currently visible or not
  • Aggregate Locks Pane: whenever a lock is engaged at an aggregate level, be it the aggregate of multiple time periods, multiple product levels, or multiple channel levels, an entry corresponding to that lock will appear in the Aggregate Locks Pane (below the Calc Toggles Pane) detailing the Product, Channel, and Time Levels where the lock exists

  • Clicking the Remove button on any of the aggregate lock entries will remove the corresponding lock in the PivotGrid, regardless of whether they are visible in the current view or not
