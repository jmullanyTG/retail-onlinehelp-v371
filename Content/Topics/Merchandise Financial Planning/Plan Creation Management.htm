Plan Creation & Management

Managing Merchandise Financial Plans within the financial planning module begins with the Financial Plan Maintenance View.  This view is where our clients will set up the structure of their plans for their target setting process.  There are three main concepts for plans that we offer to our clients.  The first concept for plans is the master plan.  The master plan is considered the “single” version of truth and can only be edited through an approval and/or merge process.  The second concept for plans is the branch plan.  The branch plan can be a copy of the master plan or a copy of a branch plan.  The branch concept is where users have the ability to make changes to their plans.  The third concept for plans is the snapshot.  Snapshots are locked down versions of a plan that cannot be edited and do not actualize.  In addition to the three main concepts for plans, we also offer as standard functionality the ability to plan based on the retail method of accounting and cost method of accounting.  Location plans can also be created and managed in this view.
