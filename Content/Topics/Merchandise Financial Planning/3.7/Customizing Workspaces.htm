Customizing Workspaces

You can customize your workspace by dragging the Hybrid Financial Planning Grid, Calculation Toggles pane, or Locks on Selected Data pane and dropping it wherever makes sense for the tasks you are performing:

  • Docked at the top/bottom/side of the window. When you drag , semi-transparent docking helpers appear on the screen. Simply drop on a helper to dock it in that helper's location. (The area where the tab/header will be docked is highlighted in blue when you hover over a docking helper.)

  • As a tab. Dropping next to an existing tab adds it as another tab in that pane.

  • On another screen. You can drag an item outside of the JustEnough window and float it wherever your prefer--even on another monitor.

Best of all, JustEnough keeps all these areas in synch no matter where they are positioned, so when you change something in one area, the effects are immediately visible in the other areas.

Saving and restoring workspaces

Once you have your workspace configured just right, you can save it (and share it) for later use. Just right-click on or next to the Hybrid Financial Planning Grid tab (or click the down arrow) to open the Workspaces context menu.

Menu Option Description
Workspaces > [Workspace Name] Opens the selected workspace.
Workspaces > Restore Default Opens the default workspace.
Workspaces > Save Default Saves the current workspace as the default workspace.
Save : [Workspace Name] Saves changes to the currently open workspace.
Delete :[Workspace Name] Deletes the current workspace.
Save New Saves the current workspace as a new workspace. You have the option of making the workspace private (only you can use it) or public (any user can use it).
