Essential Business Concepts

The following are basic conventions and examples for users to better understand how to use this component of Merchandise Financial Planning:

  • Showing/Hiding the Calc Toggles Pane: the Calc Toggles view can be collapsed by clicking the arrow at the top left adjacent the view title:

Once collapsed, the view will be visible only as a tab at the right hand side of the screen and can be restored by clicking the arrow near the view title again:

  • Selecting a Calc Toggle: to select a calc toggle, simply click the dropdown menu adjacent the desired metric, and click the calc toggle option you wish to use

  • Note: Calc Toggles become live as soon as they are selected. There is no need to click Save to enable them. The presence and enabling of the Save/Cancel buttons is a known issue in this version that will be resolved in a future release
  • Note: alternate calc toggles (meaning the toggle that is not selected by default) will be displayed in a darker gray in the pane so users can more easily identify the active toggle at a glance

  • Note: not all editable metrics have alternate calc directions, thus not all metrics are shown in the calc toggles pane; additionally, depending on the plan type, Retail or Cost, of the active plan, certain toggles may not be available (see Calc Toggle Details below for more information)
  • Hybrid Cost Dual Markdown Toggles: Receipt related calc toggles within the Hybrid Cost Dual Markdown plan type behave the same as their counterparts in the Hybrid Cost and Hybrid Retail and, thus, will not be explicitly detailed in this guide. All other toggles behave in ways that are similar but different enough to warrant further explanation.
