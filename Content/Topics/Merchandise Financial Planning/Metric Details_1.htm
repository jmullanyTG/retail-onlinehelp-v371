Metric Details

  • Reg. Sales:  Sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at original ticket or full price
  • Simple Calculation:   (Reg. Sales Units * Reg. Sales AUR)
  • LY Reg. Sales:   Last Year Sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at original ticket or full price during that period
  • Simple Calculation:   (LY Reg. Sales Units * LY Reg. Sales AUR)
  • Reg. Sales % +/- LY:   Regular sales percent increase or decrease to comparative period last year
  • Simple Calculation:   (Reg. Sales – LY Reg. Sales)/(Reg. Sales)
  • Reg. Sales Units:   Sales Units coming from all locations for products/services sold at original ticket or full price
  • Simple Calculation:   (Reg. Sales/Reg. Sales AUR)
  • LY Reg. Sales Units:  Last Year Sales Units coming from all locations for products/services sold at original ticket or full price during that period
  • Simple Calculation:   (LY Reg. Sales/LY Reg. Sales AUR)
  • Reg. Sales Units % +/- LY:   Regular Sales Units percent increase or decrease to comparative period last year
  • Simple Calculation: (Reg. Sales Units – LY Reg. Sales Units)/(Reg. Sales Units)
  • Reg. Sales AUR:   Average unit retail sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at original ticket or full price
  • Simple Calculation:   (Reg. Sales/Reg. Sales Units)
  • LY Reg. Sales AUR:   Last year average unit retail sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at original ticket or full price during that period
  • Simple Calculation:   (LY Reg. Sales/LY Reg. Sales Units)
  • Promo Sales:  Sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at a promotional price or temporary price reduction
  • Simple Calculation:   (Promo Sales Units * Promo Sales AUR)
  • LY Promo Sales:   Last Year Sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at a promotional price or temporary price reduction during that period

  • Promo Sales Units:   Sales Units coming from all locations for products/services sold at a promotional price or temporary price reduction
  • Simple Calculation:   (Promo Sales/Promo Sales AUR)
  • LY Promo Sales Units:  Last Year Sales Units coming from all locations for products/services sold at a promotional price or temporary price reduction during that period
  • Simple Calculation:   (LY Promo Sales/LY Promo Sales AUR)
  • Promo Sales Units % +/- LY:   Promotional Sales Units percent increase or decrease to comparative period last year
  • Simple Calculation: (Promo Sales Units – LY Promo Sales Units)/(Promo Sales Units)
  • Promo Sales AUR:   Average unit retail sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at a promotional price or temporary price reduction
  • Simple Calculation:   (Promo Sales/Promo Sales Units)
  • LY Promo Sales AUR:   Last year average unit retail sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at a promotional price or temporary price reduction during that period
  • Simple Calculation:   (LY Non-Comp Sales/LY Non-Comp Sales Units)
  • Markdown Sales:  Sales coming from all locations for products/services sold at a clearance price or permanent price reduction
