Essential Business Concepts

Surveys

There are several surveys that may be added to a JustEnough forecasting method including:

  • Trend Survey
  • Seasonal Trend Survey
  • Seasonal Survey
  • Intermittent Survey
  • Linear Regression Survey
  • Too Intermittent Survey
  • Trend Points
  • Reasonable Survey
  • 8 Weeks

Within each survey rule, the survey stage (either Pre-Survey or Post-Survey) must be set. Also, each individual rule will have a set of default parameters, as well as several additional parameters that may be included in order to further customize the survey, and by extension the forecast.
