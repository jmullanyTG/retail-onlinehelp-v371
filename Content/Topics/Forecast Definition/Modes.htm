Modes

There are three types of modes which will relate to the base of the forecast.

  • Static – As you project, the forecasting engine takes the most recent real data points.
  • Dynamic – The forecasting engine will take the projected data points and apply them to the forecast as if they were real data points.
  • Naïve (no confidence) – This approach will determine that the forecast for any period equals the previous period’s actual value.
