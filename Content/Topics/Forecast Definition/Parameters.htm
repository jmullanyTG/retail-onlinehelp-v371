Parameters

Most of the pre-loaded survey rules will have a few included parameters. They can be seen by clicking on the “Manage Surveys” button, and then then double clicking on the survey name. The included parameters will be displayed in a window on the right side, above a list of any potential parameters that may be included in order to customize a survey, or in the creation of a unique survey. A description of the parameter can be viewed by hovering over the parameter name.

Detailed descriptions of the included parameters are built into the tooltip function within JustEnough. As such, they may be viewed by “hovering over” the parameter with the mouse cursor.
