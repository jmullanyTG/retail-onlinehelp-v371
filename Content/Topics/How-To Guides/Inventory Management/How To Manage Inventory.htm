How To Manage Inventory Strategies

A number of factors go into creating effective and efficient inventory management strategies. These include:

  • Delivery/receiving schedules at your DCs and stores
  • Order minimums and other order constraints
  • Item priority classifications
  • Safety stock requirements
  • Lead times and replenishment cycles
  • Demand forecasts for items at individual stores

JustEnough takes all of these factors into account when making its order recommendations.

JustEnough also forecasts when inventory surpluses or shortfalls/potential stock outs may occur at your sites. You can look out for such issues, called inventory exceptions, from within JustEnough.
