Managing Store and DC Openings

This process guides you through the end-to-end process of managing demand and supply for site openings. It starts with validating site data in the system, ensures supply chains and filters are updated with the new sites, plans demand and inventory levels for the new sites, and ends with generating orders to ensure the right amount of inventory is available at your sites when they open.
