Example - Reviewing New Store Forecasts

Once forecasts have been generated for a new store--either manually or automatically as part of an overnight process--you can review the forecasts for items in your new store. In this example, we'll look at the forecasted demand for an item at a new store in Calgary

Instructions

  1. From the Manage tab, click Item Manager.
  2. In the Items dialog box, select the Forecast Review: Coats & Jackets - Retail filter.

  3. In the item document, filter the Site Name column for Calgary.

  4. Double-click on an item to display its Details pane.
  5. In the Details pane, open the Forecast Viewer tab to see the demand that JustEnough forecasted for the item at the new Calgary store, based on the store supersession created previously.
  6. You can repeat these steps for other items at the Calgary site until you are satisfied with the demand forecasting for the new store.
  7. If further revisions are required, you can adjust the store supersession and any related item supersessions as necessary. (For example, the new site may not have been added to an item supersession, resulting in no sales or forecasts for the item at Calgary.)
