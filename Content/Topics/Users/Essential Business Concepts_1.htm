Essential Business Concepts

The main screen in the roles tab will display the current roles list. Role names and descriptions are listed here, and detailed information may be accessed either by highlighting a role and clicking the “modify” button, or double clicking on the role.

  • Role Name – The name of the role. When creating new roles, it is considered best practice to keep the names generalized.
  • Description – A high level description of the role.

Much of the detailed information related to the roles is found in the “role information” window, which may be accessed by either double clicking on an existing role, or highlighting a role and clicking “modify” in the navigation pane.
