Practical Application

There are few tasks which require maintenance in the rights section of the users module. Highlighting a right and clicking on “configure rights” within the navigation pane will bring up the “right information” window.

A description of the right is available for viewing in the “right detail” tab.

Roles may be added or removed from right access within the “roles” tab.

Individual user rights may be added or restricted within the “memberships” tab.
