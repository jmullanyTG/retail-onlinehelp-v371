Allow Editing of Price Cluster

Provides access to the Add and Remove Sites buttons to modify Site Clusters in the Promotion. If the User Right “Allow Creation of Price Cluster” is granted, the User Right “Allow Editing of Price Cluster” must also be granted.

Note: This permission may be configured to be enabled or disabled during specific Promotion Workflow Status’ States
