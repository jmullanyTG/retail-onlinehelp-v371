Pricing Actions
Price Actions may be configured with the same, or a subset of, products and sites from the Pricing Plan to apply a Pricing Strategy (i.e. validations and calculations with rounding rules) to generate a new retail sale price for a specific date, forward. While the start date, products and sites will be limited by the configured Pricing Plan, they can be further excluded at the Price Action level. Currency selected within a Pricing Action will also filter the sites applied to the Pricing Action. Any manual entry entered at the Price Action or summary level will be pushed down to the individual product/site level. The solution also provides the ability to edit for specifics Site and or individual products within a Product Group.
