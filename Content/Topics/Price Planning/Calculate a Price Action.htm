Calculate a Price Action

Once the Price Action has been configured to your satisfaction, click Calculate to have the system execute the Price Strategy’s configured prioritized calculations and validations, which are run at the lowest level, and displayed as a minimum and maximum in the Price Action Summary grid.

If there are any differences in entered variable information the ‘exception’ checkbox will be marked for easy identification. To view lower level calculated pricing information, click Detail for the appropriate Variant details.

Note: You will not be able to edit the Price Action (e.g. exclusions of Variants/Sites nor enterable Price Strategy fields) while it is being calculated and validated by the system.
