DC List

The DC list will display all sites which have been designated as distribution centers, including the virtual “placeholder” DC. All DCs will be displayed, but user assign filter (inventory) roles will be applied to inventory visibility.

  • Site Name – The name of the distribution center.
  • WOC – The weeks of cover value for multi-tiered allocations supply chains. Applicable only to non-root nodes, the weeks of cover will function as a safety stock value against which the dependent stores will pull stock, to be replenished from the source or sources specified in the supply chain.
  • Warehouse Stock – Physical stock count contained within the warehouse or distribution center.
  • Available Stock – Available stock is a calculated value which determines the quantity of stock which may be allocated. Available stock is calculated as: (Stock on Hand + Inbound Stock + Placeholder Stock – Stock Reserved).
  • On Order Stock – On order stock is the summation of all supplier orders of type “pending”. These are orders which have been shipped from the supplier.
  • Late On Order Stock – Quantity of on order stock which is late.
  • Next PO In DC Date – Expected arrival date of next influx of stock from an external supplier.
  • Next PO In DC Qty – Quantity of stock in the next expected influx of stock from an external supplier.
