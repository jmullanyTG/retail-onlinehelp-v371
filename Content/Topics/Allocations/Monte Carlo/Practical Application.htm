Practical Application

System Policy

In order to enable the Monte Carlo Re-trend, the global re-trend setting must be toggled from the default PID to “MonteCarloTechnique” within the System Policy module. Users without access to this module may request the change through a super-user with appropriate environmental access or through a request to a JustEnough support engineer.

Within the System Policy module, the setting for “Forecast Re-trend Method to Use” (Allocate.Forecast.Re-trend.Method) should be set to “MonteCarloTechnique” and “Apply Changes” should be selected to enact the change.

Strategy Line

Once the re-trend method has been updated, users may elect to enable or disable re-trend at the strategy line level within Allocations. The results of the re-trended calculation may be viewed via the in-line roll-up view, or within the comprehensive roll-up view.

Technical Execution

Implementation of the Monte Carlo Re-trend mechanism requires deployment and configuration of the JustEnough R Engine.
