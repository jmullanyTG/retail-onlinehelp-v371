Metric Definitions

  • Receipts - Sum of OutstandingQuantity from tbl_SupplierOrderLine for orders that have been delivered -- spans for all time selected
  • In-Transit Stock -  Sum of OutstandingQuantity from tbl_SupplierOrderLine for orders that have been placed or are pending -- spans for all time selected
  • Unit Sales -  Sum of SalesQuantity minus returned SalesQuantity from tbl_SalesHistoryByType -- spans for historical data
  • Allocated Units - Sum of Quantity from tbl_AllocateOrder for valid engine run, non-deleted entries -- spans for all time selected
  • Reserved Stock - Sum of StockReserved + StockPending from tbl_InventoryLevel -- spans for all time selected
  • Stock on Hand (past values) - Sum of StockOnHand from tbl_ArchiveInventory
  • Stock on Hand (projected values) - Sum of BOPSOH from tbl_Allocate_Engine_Projection
  • Actionable Stock (past values) - Sum of StockOnHand from tbl_ArchiveInventory
  • Actionable Stock (current period values) - usp_Allocate_Common_AvailableToAllocate
  • Actionable Stock (projected values) - Sum of BOPSOH from tbl_Allocate_Engine_Projection
