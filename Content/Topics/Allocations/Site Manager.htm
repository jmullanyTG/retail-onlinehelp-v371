Site Manager

Store and DC ordering and receiving schedules may be set and maintained within the Site Manager module.

  • Receiving Schedule – Controls the days on which a store or DC may receive delivered stock. Orders will not be generated for any day on which stock may not be received into the location. Typically applied to stores.
  • Shipping Schedule – Controls the days on which a store or DC may ship stock out of the location. Orders will not be recommended for shipment on any day on which stock may not be shipped from a source location. Typically applied to DCs.

Schedule sets should be configured within the JustEnough Schedules Manager module. Shipping and receiving schedules may also be applied at the inventory (item-location) level within the Item Manager; inventory level schedules will override site level schedule values as they are considered an override.
