Allocate Strategy Exception Details

The allocate strategy exception details screen presents exception management through use of multiple interactive drill-through pie charts and a management grid.

  • Exception Percentage – Describes all of the inventory (item-site combination) level exceptions within the strategy. The percentages of the exceptions within the strategy will be represented within the pie chart.
  • Site Exception Areas – Once the exception type has been selected, the site groupings at which the exception is occurring will be displayed in the site exception areas field.
  • Site Details – The site details will be displayed in a grid showing the inventory level exception for the specific site or sites.
