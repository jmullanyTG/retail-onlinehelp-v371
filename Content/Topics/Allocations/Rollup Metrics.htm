Rollup Metrics

  • Site Count – Count of sites included.
  • Receipts – The sum of the delivered order quantities during the selected time span.
  • In Transit Stock – Quantity of stock which is currently in transit between sites or suppliers and sites.
  • Reserved Stock – Any stock which has been decremented and is not considered to be actionable.
  • Allocated Units – Quantity of units which have been allocated. May differ from recommended allocation in shortfall situations.
  • Unit Sales – Quantity of sales units.
  • Stock on Hand – Quantity of stock on hand.
  • Total Actionable Stock – Quantity of stock which may be allocated.
  • Recommended Allocation – Order projection based on supplied strategy parameters.
  • Demand – Projected demand based on supplied strategy parameters. Series values will not be present for middle-tier DCs (unless there is actual sales demand on the site).
  • Constrained Demand – The projected demand used to drive the ordering logic. Will include any minimums, multiples, and sub-branch demand (for middle-tier DCs) among other constraints.
  • Supplier Orders – Quantity of stock contained in orders placed with external suppliers.
  • Recommended Safety Stock – Quantity of stock considered to be the minimum acceptable level.
  • Lost Sales – Calculated missed sales opportunities based on potential stock outs.
