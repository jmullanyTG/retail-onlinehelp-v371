Price Translation Hierarchy Assignment

This screen can be accessed via Plan > Views > Currency > Price Translation Hierarchy Assignment. Once Price Translation Rules have been created, they can be assigned to levels of a selected product hierarchy, allowing for a great deal of flexibility in pricing strategies across different types of products

Important: Even if no price translations are needed, you must, at a minimum, create at least one rule with Translation Factors of 1 for all From and To currencies to be used and with a Start Date of the earliest date to be used within any of your assortment plans and assign the rule to the Total Company level of the product hierarchies used in those plans

Note: Price Translation Hierarchy Assignments work in an inheritance scheme across the selected hierarchy. This means that rules set at higher levels of the hierarchy are automatically inherited by the levels beneath unless another rule is assigned at a lower level

  • Header Area Controls
  • Hierarchy: user editable dropdown; selects the product hierarchy to which you want to assign Price Translation Rules
  • Parameters: shows/hides the Parameters area of the view
  • Execute: updates the view with a grid of all levels of the selected Hierarchy and any Assigned Rules for each
  • Price Translation Rule Hierarchy Assignments Section
  • Level Description: non-editable alpha-numeric; displays the description of the product level
  • Level Description Path: non-editable alpha-numeric; displays the full hierarchy path of the product level, with each level separated by “//”
  • Hierarchy Level Columns: non-editable alpha-numeric; displays the specific hierarchy assignment at each level; columns displayed depend on the levels in the selected Hierarchy
  • Assigned Rule: user editable dropdown; sets the Price Translation Rule to assign to the hierarchy level at the left
  • Note: new Price Translation Rules must be saved, and the view must be re-Executed for new rules to appear for selection
