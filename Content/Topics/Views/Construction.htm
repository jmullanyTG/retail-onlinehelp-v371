Construction

Chart construction contains details about how the chart is built.

  • Chart Type – The practical chart types will vary depending on the data selected and their associated dimensions. Not every chat type will make sense for every data set. Chart types include:

  • Line Stacked 100%

  • Line 3D
  • Line 3D Stacked

  • Appearance – The appearance section will let users set the color palette and the style, as well as to preview their graph.
  • Series – The series section will display all of the included data series for the chart. Depending on the chart type, there may be one or many series displayed at one time.
  • Data – The data section will describe the data points returned by the method to the chart.
