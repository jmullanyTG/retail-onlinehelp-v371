Monitoring the Replenishment Process

Once forecasts have been generated for an item, you can monitor the replenishment process for that item in the following tabs of the Item Manager:

  • Inventory Information Tab shows current stock information for the item.
  • Forecast Viewer Tab shows forecasted demand for this item.
  • Inbound and Outbound Orders Tab tab shows current orders for this item.
  • Inter-Site Projected Demand Tab shows order quantities for each location.
  • Policy Information Tab shows the replenishment strategy parameters, safety stock, and other constraints that JustEnough considers when optimizing orders.
  • Effective Policy Information Tab shows the results of the item policy on a day-by-day basis.
