Essential Business Concepts

By itself, a schedule is only used to define a date or series of dates. The behavior of the schedule is defined by the mode within the schedule set and its application to a site or a supplier.

  • Name – Schedules may be named by users. A default name of “New Schedule” will be populated when a new schedule is created.
  • Description – An optional description for the schedule.
  • Schedule Enabled – A flag which will enable or disable a schedule globally. Schedule behavior within the context of a schedule set may be controlled through the set enabled flag without affecting the schedule behavior on a global level.
  • Schedule Type – Controls the recurrence of schedules.
  • One-Time – A one-time occurrence will indicate a single date.
  • Recurring – A recurring schedule allows users to specify the frequency and the duration of a schedule.
