Technical Execution

Schedule sets are stored as “Almanacs” within the database; they may be found in tbl_Common_Schedule_Almanac. Schedule relationships are stored in tbl_Common_Schedule_AlmanacLink. This table also holds the behavior as ScheduleMode and the priority. For a particular schedule set (almanac), the priority 0 will define the highest priority, and its associated ScheduleMode will dictate the behavior of all non-specified dates.
