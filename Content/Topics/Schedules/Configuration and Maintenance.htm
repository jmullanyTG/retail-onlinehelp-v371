Configuration and Maintenance

Before schedules may be created and applied, the following data must be present, procedures run, and policy values defined:

  • Data Requirements – Schedules are not resolved based on imported calendars. Dates contained are determined by operating system parameters.
  • Maintenance Procedures – Schedules do not require specific tasks or jobs to maintain operability.
  • Cleanup – Periodically, a maintenance task should be run to clean deleted schedules and schedule sets out of the database.
  • Recommended – Annual.
  • System Policies – There are no system policies specific to schedules.
