Practical Application

Within the forecast group model management scree, users may create, edit, and delete models, as well as configure the ordering rules and view the affected inventories. Choosing “new” or “edit” will bring up the “add or update group forecast model” window.

If sales history by type data has been supplied, users may choose to include or exclude typed sales when building the model. It is highly recommended that there is some denotation in the model name and/or description.
