Advanced Settings

Users familiar with the pivot grid configuration will recognize elements of the group forecast model advanced settings screen.

Columns may be added or removed by using the “add” or “delete” buttons near the bottom of the screen. A new column will appear (as a new row) at the bottom of the grid and may be reordered if necessary.

If the column is an expression column, the expression may be manually entered by clicking on the expression drop-down icon.
