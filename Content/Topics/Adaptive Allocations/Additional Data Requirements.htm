Additional Data Requirements

Price and Cost

For customers using JustEnough Price Management, the price and cost conversion calculations will read information from tbl_InventoryPrice and tbl_InventoryCost (Where PriceType/CostType = system policy values, default to 1) if there is data available; this may also be imported. If this data is unavailable, the conversion calculations will be based on the imported values for DefaultCostPrice and DefaultSellingPrice.
