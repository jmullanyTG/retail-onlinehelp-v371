Adaptive Allocations Overview

Adaptive Allocations is a new extension of the JustEnough Allocation solution that automates the creation and allocation of localized assortments. Adaptive Allocations determines an optimal mix of products utilizing a revolutionary draft engine and data analytics for locations and products to fulfill a need that can be set through financial planning, location planning and other cover methods.

Adaptive Allocations can bridge the gap in Inventory Management between a full strategic approach to allocating products to locations and the dispersal miscellaneous (e.g. broken size run, leftover product, etc.) products through allocations groups.

While Adaptive Allocations shares many concepts with Assortment Planning, it is in no way meant to replace Assortment Planning; the additional complexity and detail required to properly execute Assortment Planning will provide a higher degree of precision in the final output.

In order to create allocation orders which may be executed within the JustEnough Order Manager, the Adaptive Allocations process will require users to create a demand plan from an array of available sources, which will in turn drive the choice count process as well as the overall output volumes of the scenario plan. Additionally, a product set comprised of the desired products to be allocated will be configured and any attributes associated with those products should be selected for inclusion and their priorities weighted.

Together, the JustEnough Draft Engine will use the store level need targets and choice counts to select the optimal products based on attribute affinity and in the appropriate volumes.
