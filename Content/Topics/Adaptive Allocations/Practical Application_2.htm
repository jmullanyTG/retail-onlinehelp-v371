Practical Application

Creation of choice count models is managed within the choice count screen. Like all management screens within the Adaptive Allocations module, it may be undocked from JustEnough and realigned or moved to a secondary monitor by clicking and dragging the tab to the desired location.

Users may create and configure choice count models for consumption during the scenario plan building phase within the choice count model grid.

New plans may be created by selecting “New”.

Users will be required to fill in all fields before saving. Access level will default to “public” unless otherwise specified.

Choice count models (even linked choice count models) may be deleted for users with appropriate permissions by highlighting row and selecting “Delete”.
