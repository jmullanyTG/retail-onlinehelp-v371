Location Plan

Within Location Planning, financial targets are created for levels of the product hierarchy down to store level. The creation of a location plan based demand plan within Adaptive Allocations, the mechanism is similar to that of strategic financial planning, but without the need of a product model, as store level indexing is not necessary.
