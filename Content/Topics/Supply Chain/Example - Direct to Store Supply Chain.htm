Example - "Direct To Store Delivery" Supply Chain

This example shows a direct-to-store supply chain where the inventory source for a store is the external supplier.

Workflow

Click to view a diagram of a direct-to-store supply chain.

Instructions

  1. From the Maintain tab, click Supply Chain.

    Note: If the Open Supply Chain dialog box does not automatically appear, click Open Supply Chain on the toolbar.

  2. In the Open Supply Chain dialog box, select the [Supplier - DC/Store] supply chain.

  3. In the supply chain document, select a store site in the Hierarchy pane. Notice that it's source (displayed in the Alternate Source gird) is External Supplier.

Results

With this supply chain, JustEnough will:

  1. Calculate projected demand versus projected stock on hand for each item at each site.
  2. Generate orders as needed from the external supplier for each item at each location.
  3. Have those orders delivered directly from the supplier to each site.
