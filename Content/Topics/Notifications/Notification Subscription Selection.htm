Notification Subscription Selection

System Administrators may select all or a subset of notifications to communicate to end users, for each solution, as well as it’s default delivery method.

Marking the checkbox at the solution level (e.g. Allocations, Assortment, Inventory Forecast, Prepack, etc.) will subscribe to all of the Notifications associated to it.

By default, the system will mark any subscribed to Notification with a ‘soft’ delivery method.
