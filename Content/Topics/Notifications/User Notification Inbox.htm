User Notification Inbox

The purpose of the Notification Icon and Inbox is to provide you with a method of communication from the system that a process they initiated (e.g. a long running calculation or forecast) has been submitted, completed, cancelled, expired or failed.

The Notification Inbox can be accessed by clicking the Notification icon found at the top right. The notification icon will also display the number of unread notifications.

You may dismiss Notifications that no longer are relevant thus removing them from immediate view. The dismissed notifications will be kept for a period of time and can be viewed by clicking the History toggle before they are purged by a schedule process.

The Notification Inbox can be moved to another screen, if needed, and sorted by Date/Time by default. Unread notifications will be highlighted.
