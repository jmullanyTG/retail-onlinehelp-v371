Allocation

The following enhancements are now available in the Allocation module.

Product groups now built from allocation strategies

Product groups are now composed of allocation strategies instead of variants. The major impact of this change is that omni-channel organizations can now create channel-specific product groups, which was not previously possible.  Before, adding a variant to a product group meant all of the allocation strategies associated with that variant were automatically and irrevocably included in that group. Now, for example, if an item has a different allocation strategy per channel, a user can choose just the specific strategies they want to include in a product group. The strategies that are not included in the product group will remain available for other uses.

The steps for creating and maintaining product groups in the Allocations module do not change as a result of this enhancement. What has changed in the UI is that the grid at the bottom of the Product Groups pane now shows eligible allocation strategies instead of eligible variants.

New fields in the grid include:

  • Allocation Strategy ID 
  • Eligible Channel
  • Location Strategy

Note: To align existing product groups to the new architecture, this enhancement requires a migration step during upgrade. See Breaking Changes for more information.
