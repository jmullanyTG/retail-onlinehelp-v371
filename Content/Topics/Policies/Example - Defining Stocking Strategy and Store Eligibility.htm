Example - Defining Stocking Strategy and Store Eligibility

Policy tasks apply replenishment and stocking strategy-related settings to the items contained in the task's filter.

In this example, we'll create 3 policy tasks to support the introduction a new item, "High Collar Angelina Jacket" to stores:

  • A policy task that sets the default DC stocking strategy for the new item.
  • A policy task that sets the default store stocking strategy for the new item.
  • A policy task that excludes stores outside of the USA and Canada from receiving this item.

Workflow

Click to view the Managing Stocking Strategies workflow.

Instructions

1. Create a policy task for DCs.
  1. From the Manage tab, click Policy.
  2. In the Policy Task toolbar, click New.

  3. In the Global tab of the Add Policy Task dialog box, enter the name: New Item Management (DC).

  4. In the Filter field, click Change and choose the DC All Items - Stocking Strategy filter.

  5. In the Policy tab, enter the following values:

    • Order Policy Type: Normal Stocked
    • Review Period Days: 1
    • Replenishment Cycle Days: 30
    • Safety Stock Days: 21
  6. Click OK to create the policy task.
 2. Create a policy task for stores.
  1. From the Manage tab, click Policy.
  2. In the Policy Task toolbar, click New.

  3. In the Add Policy Task dialog box, enter the name: New Item Management (Store).

  4. In the Filter field, click Change and choose the New Item Policy Management: Stores filter.

  5. In the Policy tab, enter the following values:

    • Order Policy Type: Normal Stocked
    • Review Period Days: 1
    • Replenishment Cycle Days: 7
    • Safety Stock Days: 14
    • Minimum Safety Stock Units: 2
  6. Click OK to create the policy task.
 3. Create a store eligibility policy task.
  1. From the Manage tab, click Policy.
  2. In the Policy Task toolbar, click New.

  3. In the Add Policy Task dialog box, enter the name: Store Eligibility: High Collar Angelina Jacket Only Active for Canada and USA.

  4. In the Filter field, click Change and choose the High Collar Angelina Jacket Store Eligibility filter.

  5. In the Policy tab, enter the following values:

    • Order Policy Type: Redundant
  6. Click OK to create the policy task.

Results

Once created, each policy task appears in the Policy screen.
