Generating Supplier Orders

Supplier orders are orders that JustEnough recommends placing with your suppliers to ensure there is enough inventory in your sites to meet anticipated demand. In a typical supply chain, supplier orders are used to stock DCs. Distribution orders can then be generated to distribute inventory to stores.

Setup

The following objects should be set up before generating supplier orders:

  • A filter that includes the items and/or sites that you want to generate orders for.
  • The supply chain that you want to use.
  • Demand forecasts for the items you want to distribute.
  • Stocking strategies for the items you want to distribute.

Instructions

  1. From the Manage tab, click Order Manager.
  2. In the Order toolbar, click Supplier.

  3. In the Items dialog box, select the filter with the items and sites you want to generate orders for.

  4. In the Add Supplier Order dialog box, enter the parameters for the order.

  5. Click OK.

  6. Once JustEnough calculates the recommended orders, they appear in the Order screen. Each row represents a separate recommended order, and contains basic details of the order.

  7. Double-click on an order row to view the demand forecast, stocking strategy, stock holding projection, and other details that drove the order recommendation.

  8. Proceed according to your company's order authorization workflow.

Results

The order follows your company's authorization workflow. Once authorized, JustEnough sends the order to your ERP for processing.
