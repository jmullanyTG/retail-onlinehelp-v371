Spend X Get Amount Discount Promotion Strategy specific Calculations

Calculation Name

Configuration

EDLP – Promo Low -  Item

Set the LowestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the LowestRetailPrice.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – LowestPromotionPrice = LowestRetailPrice

EDLP – Promo High -  Item

Set the HighestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the HighestRetailPrice.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – HighestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – HighestPromotionPrice = HighestRetailPrice
