BOGO AA - Filldown - Buy Quantity - Item

Fill down the keyed buy quantity to all levels while checking for any buy quantity overrides and filling those down as needed. To be used in an ItemMaster implementation.

  • Group Type – Buy Get Group

BOGO AA Free – Promo Low -  Item

  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – LowestPromotionPrice = LowestRetailPrice
