Multi - Promo Low - Item

Set the LowestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the total promotional price divided by the multi buy quantity. Overrides are not configured for multi buy Promotion Strategies because it is not possible to accurately calculate individual product Promotion Price with overrides.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – LowestPromotionPrice = (CASE WHEN  ISNULL(MultiBuyQuantity,0) <> 0 THEN (( ISNULL(TotalPromotionalPrice,0))/ ISNULL(MultiBuyQuantity,0)) ELSE 0 END)
