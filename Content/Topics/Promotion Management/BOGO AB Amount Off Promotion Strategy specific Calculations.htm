BOGO Amount Off - Low - Item

Set the LowestDollarDiscount field at the Item level to the BOGO amount off override if available, otherwise the keyed BOGO amount off.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestDollarDiscount
  • Formula – LowestDollarDiscount = ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO Amount Off - Low - Variant

Set the Variant level LowestDollarDiscount field to the minimum Item level BogoDollarDiscount_Override, if available, or BogoDollarDiscount. To be used in a Variant implementation.

  • Calculation Level – Variant Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestDollarDiscount
  • Aggregation Type – MIN
  • Formula – ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO Amount Off - High - Variant

  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – HighestDollarDiscount

  • Formula – ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO Amount Off - Low - Group

BOGO Amount Off - Low - Promo Aggr

Set the Promotion Aggregate level LowestDollarDiscount field to the minimum BogoDollarDiscount_Override, if available, or BogoDollarDiscount of the products (Item, Variant or groups) in the Promotion. For example, if the Promotion consists of Items and groups, the Promotion Aggregate value is set by comparing the minimum BogoDollarDiscount at Item level for the Items, the minimum BogoDollarDiscount at the Group level for all groups, then setting to the Promotion Aggregate LowestDollarDiscount to the minimum value of all, taking overrides into account at every level.

  • Calculation Level – Promotion Aggregate Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestDollarDiscount
  • Aggregation Type – MIN
  • Formula – ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO Amount Off - High - Promo Aggr

Set the Promotion Aggregate level HighestDollarDiscount field to the maximum BogoDollarDiscount_Override, if available, or BogoDollarDiscount of the products (Item, Variant or Groups) in the Promotion. For example, if the Promotion consists of Variants and groups, the Promotion Aggregate value is set by comparing the maximum BogoDollarDiscount at Variant level for the Variants, the maximum BogoDollarDiscount at the Group level for all groups, then setting to the Promotion Aggregate HighestDollarDiscount to the maximum value of all, taking overrides into account at every level.

  • Calculation Level – Promotion Aggregate Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group

  • Formula – ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO AB - Amount Off - Get - Promo Low – Item

Set the LowestPromotionPrice field at the Get Group Item level to the lowest retail price minus the amount off (or override amount off, if entered).

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – LowestPromotionPrice = ISNULL(LowestRetailPrice,0) -  ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO AB - Amount Off - Get - Promo High – Item

Set the HighestPromotionPrice field at the Get Group Item level to the highest retail price minus the amount off (or override amount off, if entered).

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Get Group
  • Calculation Field – HighestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – HighestPromotionPrice = ISNULL(HighestRetailPrice,0) - ISNULL(BogoDollarDiscount_Override,BogoDollarDiscount)

BOGO AB - Buy - Promo Low - Item

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
