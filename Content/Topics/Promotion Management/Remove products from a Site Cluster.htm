Remove products from a Site Cluster

To remove Item Masters/Variants from a Promotion Site Cluster:

  1. Navigate to Item Group under Promotion Group which will automatically display the individual products.
  2. Select the checkbox next to the appropriate products and click Remove.
  3. Click Yes in the window to confirm that you want to remove the selected products from the Promotion Site Cluster.
  4. The updates will automatically be saved to the Promotion.

To remove one or more products from Item Groups /Variant Groups from the Promotion:

  1. Navigate to the product group you want to modify under Promotion Group and click Edit which will cause the Edit Group window to display.
  2. Select the product group with the dates of the Promotion and click on the black hammer icon to modify the Promotion Filter.
  3. Select ‘Update The Following Filter’, ensure the Filter with the dates of the Promotion is selected and click Ok.
  4. Modify the Filter as desired and click Ok.

  5. The Item Group/Variant Group will be updated in the Promotion.

    Note: Highlighting the Promotion Group and clicking Remove will delete all SKUs from the Promotion. Be very cautious of performing this action.
