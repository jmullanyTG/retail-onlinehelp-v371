Fixed Promotion Strategy specific Calculations

Calculation Name

Configuration

Fixed – Filldown – Item

Fill down the keyed promotion price to all levels while checking for any promotion price overrides and filling those down as needed. To be used in an ItemMaster implementation.

  • Method – Promotion_PriceCalculation_OverrideColumn_Filldown_ItemMaster
  • Calculation Level – Group Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – PromotionPrice

Fixed – Filldown – Variant

Fill down the keyed promotion price to all levels while checking for any promotion price overrides and filling those down as needed. To be used in a Variant implementation.

  • Method – Promotion_PriceCalculation_OverrideColumn_Filldown_Variant
  • Calculation Level – Group Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – PromotionPrice

Fixed - Low - Item

Set the LowestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the promotion price override if available, otherwise the keyed promotion price.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – LowestPromotionPrice =  ISNULL(PromotionPrice_Override,PromotionPrice)

Fixed - High - Item

 

Set the HighestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the promotion price override if available, otherwise the keyed promotion price.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – HighestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – HighestPromotionPrice = ISNULL(PromotionPrice_Override,PromotionPrice)
