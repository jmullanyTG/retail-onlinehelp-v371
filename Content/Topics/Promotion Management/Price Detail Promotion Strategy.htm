Price Detail - Promotion Strategy

The Price Detail screen will vary depending on the Promotion Strategy selected. The display grid left pane should be used to enter Promotion level pricing that will be the default for all products in the Site Cluster. By clicking Save the changes will be applied to the pivot grid in the right pane.

The Price Details pivot grid will display customized columns based on the specified Promotion Strategy. This grid provides the ability to override pricing for one or more products for the specified Site Cluster. Modify the appropriate columns in the grid and click Save. Then click the diskette icon to Save the Promotion.

  1. The display grid in the left pane will allow you to enter pricing data for the selected Site Cluster and click on Save in the left pane to apply to all products in the Site Cluster.
  2. If pricing overrides are needed at the product level for the Site Cluster, they can be made in the pane on the right and will be saved after clicking on Save in the right pane.
  3. The information to be entered will differ based on the Promotion Strategy selected.
