Percent off Promotion Strategy Calculation Set

Fill down the keyed percent off from the Promotion level to all levels. At the Item level, set the LowestPercentDiscount and HighestPercentDiscount equal to the keyed percent off. Roll up these percent off fields from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

Calculate and set the LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice at the Item level. Roll up LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

  1. *Percent Off - Filldown - Item
  2. ** Percent Off - Filldown - Variant
  3. Percent Off - Low - Item
  4. Percent Off - High - Item
  5. ** Percent Off - Low - Variant
  6. ** Percent Off - High - Variant
  7. Percent Off - Low - Group
  8. Percent Off - High - Group
  9. Percent Off - Low - Promo Aggr
  10. Percent Off - High - Promo Aggr
  11. Percent Off - Promo Low - Item
  12. Percent Off - Promo High - Item
  13. **Standard - Promo Low - Variant
  14. **Standard - Promo High - Variant
  15. Standard - Promo Low - Group
  16. Standard - Promo High - Group
  17. Standard - Promo Low - Promo Aggr
  18. Standard - Promo High - Promo Aggr

*Item fill down Calculation is only included in an Item Master implementation and can be omitted for a Variant implementation.

**Variant Calculations are only included in a Variant implementation and can be omitted for an Item Master implementation.
