BOGO AA Promotion Price Promotion Strategy Calculation Set

Fill down the keyed BOGO buy quantity, get quantity and promotion price from the Promotion level to all levels. At the Item level, set the LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice equal to the keyed BOGO promotion price checking for an override. Roll up these promotion price fields from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.
