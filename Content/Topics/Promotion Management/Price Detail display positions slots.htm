Price Detail – display positions (slots)

The second tab of the Price Detail screen allows you to assign the Promotion directly into an open Slot in an Event Slot Set. Ensure the Parameters button is enabled. To load all available Slots, select ‘All’ from the Available Events View Option and click Execute.

The grid will then display a row for every Slot contained in an Event that has overlapping Sale Dates and some Sites in common with the current Promotion.

A checkmark in the ‘Assigned’ column signifies that the current Promotion has already been placed in that Slot. Event Slots do not appear in the grid, signify that either a different Promotion has been placed the Slot or you do not have access to the Space that the Slot is in. A Slot may be displayed across more than one row if the Promotion in the Slot is not associated with all Geographies in the Event.

The ‘Available Events View Option’ dropdown list determines which accessible Slots from overlapping Events are displayed in the grid. Select one of the options below and click Execute to alter the Slots displayed in the grid:

  • All – Show Slots that are available or assigned to the current Promotion.
  • Available – Show Slots that do not have Promotions assigned to all Event Geographies.
  • Assigned – Show Slots that the current Promotion has been assigned to.

To assign the Promotion to an available Event Slot, place a checkmark in the ‘Assigned’ column of the applicable row in the grid, click Save and click Ok in the window.

Once a Promotion has been assigned to a Slot, the pane on the right will load to display all of the Products and Product Groups associated with the Promotion.

Selecting the ‘Featured’ checkbox beside one Product (Item or Group) in this pane will apply the selected Product’s Master Content and the Pricing to the Event’s Slot.

Click Save, above the right pane, to apply the Content and Pricing selections to the Event Slot. The ability to push a specific Product’s Content and pricing is not available for BOGO AB type Promotions.
