Modify a Promotion

When a new Promotion is saved or an existing Promotion opened, the system will display a new tab with the Promotion Detail screen.  This tab will allow you, if you have the appropriate rights, to modify all aspects of the Promotion.

The Promotion screen is divided into two main sections: the upper portion will display the list of Promotion Site Clusters with their assigned Promotion Strategies which allows you to tailor the Promotion for specific sets of Sites as needed. It also includes several buttons to allow you to modify the Sites assigned to the Site Clusters for the Promotion.

The lower portion will display the selected Site Cluster’s products, pricing details, bids (if configured), companion items (if configured), and analysis. This allows you to modify the promotional products and pricing details to be specific for each Site Cluster. In the left pane on the Site Cluster Items and Price Details tabs, you may view or edit Promotional Pricing for the selected Site Cluster. When applied, those changes will affect the pricing data in the right pane of the Price Details tab.
