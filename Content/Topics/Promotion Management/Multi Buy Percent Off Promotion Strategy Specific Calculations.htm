Multi Percent Off - Percent Off - Low - Promo Aggr

Set the Promotion Aggregate level LowestPercentDiscount field to the minimum PercentDiscount of the products (Item, Variant or groups) in the Promotion. For example, if the Promotion consists of Items and groups, the Promotion Aggregate value is set by comparing the minimum PercentDiscount at Item level for the Items, the minimum PercentDiscount at the Group level for all groups, then setting to the Promotion Aggregate LowestPercentDiscount to the minimum value of all.

  • Calculation Level – Promotion Aggregate Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPercentDiscount
  • Aggregation Type – MIN
  • Formula – ISNULL(PercentDiscount,0)

Multi Percent Off - Percent Off - High - Promo Aggr

For example, if the Promotion consists of Variants and groups, the Promotion Aggregate value is set by comparing the maximum PercentDiscount at Variant level for the Variants, the maximum PercentDiscount at the Group level for all groups, then setting to the Promotion Aggregate HighestPercentDiscount to the maximum value of all.

  • Calculation Level – Promotion Aggregate Level

Multi Percent Off - Promo Low - Item

Set the LowestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the lowest retail price minus the proportioned amount off. Overrides are not configured for multi buy Promotion Strategies because it is not possible to accurately calculate individual product Promotion Price with overrides.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – LowestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – LowestPromotionPrice = ISNULL(LowestRetailPrice,0) - (ISNULL(PercentDiscount_Override,PercentDiscount) * ISNULL(LowestRetailPrice,0))

Multi Percent Off - Promo High - Item

Set the HighestPromotionPrice field at the Item level to the highest retail price minus the proportioned amount off. Overrides are not configured for multi buy Promotion Strategies because it is not possible to accurately calculate individual product Promotion Price with overrides.

  • Calculation Level – Item Level
  • Group Type – Buy Get Group
  • Calculation Field – HighestPromotionPrice
  • Formula – HighestPromotionPrice = ISNULL(HighestRetailPrice,0) - (ISNULL(PercentDiscount_Override,PercentDiscount) * ISNULL(HighestRetailPrice,0))
