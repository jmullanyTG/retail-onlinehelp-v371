Companion items

The Companion Items tab provides the ability to promote products that are not on the Promotion. Some examples of Companion Items are batteries for promoted electronic devices, extended warranties, cell phone cases and car chargers. Companion Item associations are independent of Site Cluster definitions.

To add a Companion Item, click on New to display the products Filter window. You may select from an existing Filter or click on the black hammer icon to create a custom Filter.

Click Ok when finished selecting products. The Companion Items will be added to the grid. Comments may be added directly into the grid if needed.
