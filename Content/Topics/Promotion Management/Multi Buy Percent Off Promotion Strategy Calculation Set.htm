Multi Buy Percent Off Promotion Strategy Calculation Set

Fill down keyed total percent off and multi buy quantity from the Promotion level to all levels. At the Item level, set the LowestPercentDiscount and HighestPercentDiscount equal to the keyed percent off. Roll up these percent off fields from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

Calculate and set the LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice at the Item level. Roll up LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.
