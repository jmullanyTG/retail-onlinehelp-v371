Fixed Promotion Strategy Calculation Set

Fill down the keyed promotion price from the Promotion level to all levels. Set the Item level LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice equal to the keyed promotion price. Roll up LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

  1. *Fixed - Filldown - Item
  2. **Fixed - Filldown - Variant
  3. Fixed - Low - Item
  4. Fixed - High - Item
  5. **Standard - Promo Low - Variant
  6. **Standard - Promo High - Variant
  7. Standard - Promo Low - Group
  8. Standard - Promo High - Group
  9. Standard - Promo Low - Promo Aggr
  10. Standard - Promo High - Promo Aggr

* Item fill down Calculation is only included in an Item Master implementation and can be omitted for a Variant implementation.

**Variant Calculations are only included in a Variant implementation and can be omitted for an Item Master implementation.
