Amount off Promotion Strategy Calculation Set

Fill down the keyed amount off from the Promotion level to all levels. At the Item level, set the LowestDollarDiscount and HighestDollarDiscount equal to the keyed amount off. Roll up these amount off fields from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

Calculate and set the LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice at the Item level. Roll up LowestPromotionPrice and HighestPromotionPrice from the Item level to the Promotion Aggregate level.

  8. Amount Off - High - Group
