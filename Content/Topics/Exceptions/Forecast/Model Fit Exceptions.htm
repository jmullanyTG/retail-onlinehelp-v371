Model Fit Exceptions

The Model Fit exception flags level forecasts where the MAPE (Mean Average Percentage Error) calculated by the Forecast engine is less than the specified threshold for acceptable model fit.

Filter conditions

To filter for forecasts with Model Fit exceptions:

From the Level Forecast Exceptions field group:

    • Field LevelMethodFit is smaller or equal to 30 (or whatever percentage value you want to be the threshold).
    • Field LevelForecastReviewed is False.
