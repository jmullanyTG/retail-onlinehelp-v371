Lost Sales Exceptions

The Lost Sales exception flags forecasts when lost sales due to stock outs are detected in a previous period (as calculated by JustEnough).

Filter conditions

To filter for forecasts with Lost Sales exceptions:

From the Inventory Forecast Exceptions field group:

    • Field LostSalesDetection is True.
    • Field LevelForecastReviewed is False.
