Calendar

The Calendar View displays a Gantt chart view of all Campaigns and Events. Campaign colors are selected when a Campaign is created, whereas the Event Type dictates Event colors. By double clicking a Campaign, a window will open with the Campaign Header for review or edit. Double clicking an Event, within the Calendar, will open a window with the Event Header for review or edit.

The Date Display Type drop down list provides the ability to view Events by their Ad Dates or their Sale Dates. The Time Scale ‘+’ and ‘–‘ buttons can be used to see Campaigns and Events displayed by time of day, day, week, month, quarter or year.
