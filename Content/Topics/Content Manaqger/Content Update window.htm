Content Update window

When Save is clicked to save the updated Master Content in the Content Details pane and the product is included in a future Promotion or Event, the Content Update window is displayed.

In the window, you can choose to push Master Content (i.e. Copy Text, Images or both) to the following:

  • Promotion Content – Future Promotions containing the product will be listed here. Selecting one or more Promotions will overwrite the Promotion Content with the updated Master Content.
  • Event Content – Future Events containing Promotions with the product will be listed here. Selecting one or more Event will overwrite the Event Content for the Promotion with the updated Master Content.

Click Ok to save and update the selected Content instances.
