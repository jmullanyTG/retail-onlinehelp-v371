Content Manager Home Screen

The Content Manager home screen displays four panes that provide the ablity to manage products, product groups and their respective Master Content.

  • Products Pane – The Products pane, in the top left of the screen, displays all Item Masters or Variants.
  • Product Groups Pane – The Product Groups pane, in the bottom left of the screen, displays all groupings of products. Double clicking on a product group loads both the Content Details and Product Group Details panes.
  • Content Details Pane – The Content Details pane displays all Master Content Versions of Copy Text and Images for products. This pane only appears when a product is is in ‘Edit’ mode. Each Master Content Version may have unique Copy Text fields.
  • Item Master or Variant – Item Masters and Variants are edited via the Edit button or by double clicking the product in the Products pane.
  • Product Groups – Item Master groups, Variant groups, and groupings of groups  are edited via the Edit button or by double clicking the product in the Product Groups pane.
  • Product Group Details Pane – The Product Group Details pane only appears when a Variant or product group is edited via the Edit button or by double clicking it in the Products pane or Product Groups pane respectively. In the case an Item Master is selected in the Products pane, the Product Group Details pane is hidden. In all other cases the contents of this pane vary depending on the type of product selected:

  • Item Master – The Product Group Details pane is hidden as there are no child prodcuts that are associated to it.

  • Item Master Group – Displays the Item Filter used to create the group. Clicking View Items will display the associated child products.

  • Variant – Displays the Item Masters that comprise the Variant.

  • Variant Group – Displays the Variants in the group. Clicking View Variants will display Variant Group and Filter.

  • Grouping of Product Groups – Displays the product groups within the grouping.
