JustEnough Forecast Tournament

During a forecasting task, JustEnough’s powerful forecasting engine generates forecasts using a variety of forecasting methods and, based on the rules outlined in the Forecast Definition module, compares these methods against one another. The methods which are able to provide a series which meets all of the specified rules/requirements will be ranked by method fit with the highest method fit being displayed.

It is typical for the forecasting computations to be run as part of an overnight process (commonly a weekly task) for all items, and then managed by exception individually. Forecasts may be re-run for individual items from within the forecast viewer using the same tournament concept, however, certain methods may be excluded or locked for individual forecasts.
