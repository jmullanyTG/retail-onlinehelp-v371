Practical Application

For most users, interaction with the forecasting module will involve the review and approval of forecasts. Users will, upon entry of the forecasts module, be presented with the need to select a filter.

By selecting the check box, users may choose some items from the filter instead of the entire filter. Clicking the “tool” button will launch the “maintain filters” window from the JustEnough filters module.

The full view of the forecast view will display the graph, grid and details in a pre-defined layout.

 

 

 

 

Each of these views may be shown or hidden by toggling the graph, grid, and details buttons in the upper right.

The action buttons within the ribbon at the top of the forecast viewer will present users with most of the actions they may want to take in order to adjust anything pertinent to the forecast.

“Forecast”, “Notes”, and “Causals” will launch new windows, and “Item Manager” will launch the JustEnough Item Manager module.

“Forecast” will re-forecast the inventory item. It launches a window similar to the one seen when forecasting an item for the first time.

“Notes” will launch the notes viewer, which can be an important tool when approving or manipulating forecast values.

“Causals” will launch the “maintain item causal links” window.
