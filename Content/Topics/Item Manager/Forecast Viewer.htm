Forecast Viewer

The forecast viewer tab will display identical information to that which may be found in the Forecasts Module. Additional information may be found within the Forecasts Module help documentation.

Allocation vs Replenishment forecasts

The Forecast Viewer tab displays allocation- or replenishment-related forecast data depending on whether the selected item is managed by the Allocations module or the

Demand Forecasting/Inventory Planning/Replenishment suite ("Replenishment", for short).

If you have both the Allocations module and the Replenishment suite enabled in your instance of JustEnough, the Allocation Strategy Selection field in the Allocation Strategy screen sets whether an item is managed by Allocations or Replenishment. You can add the Execution Process column to your Item Manager view to see which process manages each item. Its read-only values are "Allocations engine" or "Replenishment engine".
