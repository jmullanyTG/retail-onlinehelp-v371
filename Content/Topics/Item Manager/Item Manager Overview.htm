Item Manager Overview

Item Manager is a powerful, yet flexible tool for monitoring and managing the inventory planning process at the inventory (item/location) level. It is a central hub of information within JustEnough, helping you to quickly drive inventory-related decisions. Within the Item Manager, you can build reports, manage inventory-level policies, and more.

Filter-driven item management

The most powerful feature within JustEnough is undoubtedly the ability to streamline workflow processes through the concept of management by exception. By using dynamic exception filters within Item Manager, you can quickly identify items which require your attention and go to work on them immediately. Using dynamic filters should be an important everyday task for demand planners, buyers, and supply chain managers. You can even tie these filters to a dashboard, making it easy to prioritize your work in JustEnough.

Centralized information

Item Manager is a one-stop shop for inventory-related information about your items, displaying information from your ERP system as well as other JustEnough modules. Available information includes (but is not limited to):

  • Inventory information such as inventory ID, stock on hand, and cost price.
  • Demand forecasts for the item (from the Forecast module).
  • Inventory-specific inbound and outbound orders for the item.
  • Inter-site projected demand for the item
  • Supply information such as lead time, order multiple, min/max quantities, and alternate suppliers.
  • Inventory policy parameters for the item (from the Policy module)
  • Status information
  • Stock holding projections
  • Multi-site inventory at the item master level
  • Financial planning information (from the Merchandise Financial Planning module)

Customizable views

A wealth of information is available within Item Manager, but you probably won't want to see it all at once. With flexible field selectors and powerful pivoting capabilities, .you can quickly create and save custom views that enable you o focus on just the information related to your tasks.

Life cycle management

You can perform a myriad of tasks within Item Manager, including managing the phase-in and phase-out dates of your items. JustEnough's ordering engine uses these dates to optimize its recommended purchase orders at each end of an item's life cycle, calibrating order quantities accordingly.
