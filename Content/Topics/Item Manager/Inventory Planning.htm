Inventory Planning

Criticality Description

Criticalities are user-applied indicators that are used to differentiate the functional importance of products such as a low cost item that is critical to the sale of a high cost item, but does not have a significant investment value.  Criticalities can be used to set bin levels by service level per criticality and minimum safety stock values per criticality.  Update during data import or by making the field editable.
