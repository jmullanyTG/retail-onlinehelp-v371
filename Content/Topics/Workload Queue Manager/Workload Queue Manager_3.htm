Workload Queue Historical Statuses

The following Statuses will be seen in the Workload Queue History list

  • Completed - The Batch has completed execution, without error, and will be removed from the ‘active’ Workload Queue and can now be referenced in the History Logs.
  • Completed With Errors - The Batch has completed execution, with error, and will be removed from the ‘active’ Workload Queue and can now be referenced in the History Logs.
  • Failed - The Batch could not be processed by the Workload Queue. The system will remove the Batch from the ‘active’ Workload Queue and can now be referenced in the History Logs.
  • Expired - The Batch expired before execution could begin. The system will remove the Batch from the ‘active’ Workload Queue and can now be referenced in the History Logs.
  • Removed - The Batch was removed. The system will remove the Batch from the ‘active’ Workload Queue and can now be referenced in the History Logs.
