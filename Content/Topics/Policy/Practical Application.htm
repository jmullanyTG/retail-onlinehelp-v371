Practical Application

The main screen encountered upon entering the inventory policy module will display the current policy tasks and their respective details.

Policy tasks are sorted by their respective execution orders in the policy task grid.

They may be reordered by clicking on the “Reorder” button in the ribbon. This will launch the “Reorder Policy Tasks” window.

To reorder a task, users should highlight the specific task and click the up or down arrows on the right hand side. This will change the task execution order. Changes may also be made by editing the task execution order field (changes will reorder and normalize on save).

Tasks may be executed outside of the daily job manager by clicking the “execute” button. This may be necessary if changes have been made to existing enabled policy tasks.
