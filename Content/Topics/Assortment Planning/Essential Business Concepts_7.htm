Essential Business Concepts

The reconciliation screen is part of the standard Assortment Planning module and can be found in the Assortment section of the software. It contains information specific to Assortment Planning. The information it contains should be utilized to soft reconcile the aggregation of the plans within the assortment module to levels of commonality within Merchandise Financial Planning as it pertains to the product, location and time hierarchies.  Soft reconcile is a comparison of one set of plans against another for key metrics such as Sales, Sales Units, Cost of Sales, AUR, AUC, Margin, Margin %, Inventory in cost and units and receipts.

The Reconciliation Report contains all the foundational metrics related to the aggregation of sales, margin, receipt and inventory data up the product and location hierarchies defined in the active assortment plan.  Users have the ability to review future period plans and actualized data as well.   Additionally, one of the key components of the Reconciliation is the enablement of users to review the aggregated assortment plan data vs. financial targets set for periods within a selected merchandise financial plan.
