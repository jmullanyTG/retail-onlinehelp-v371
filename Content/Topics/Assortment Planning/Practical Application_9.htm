Practical Application

This report can be used to slice and dice the assortment for a defined product and site hierarchy levels and window of time and to pass key information to users that do not have access to the system. For example, merchandise planners can export this report to a PDF or Excel to share with merchants or buyers. Additionally, this report is intended to provide insight to how an assortment is planned and how items within are performing against those plans through the utilization of the comparison plan metrics.  Users can evaluate their plans at aggregated levels vs. last yearâ€™s performance at the same comparable level. 

This tool can be useful when users need to speak to their assortment plans at specific levels and can provide insight into areas with too much inventory vs. demand, or to evaluate pricing and cost strategies against the profit margin requirements.

Further uses include evaluating inventory adjustments or movements across channels, where shrink is occurring in the business, etc.  Users can also evaluate their aggregate receipt plan strategy vs. units already on order.  This report is integral to the evaluation and analysis of an assortment.

Note:  This report is a pivot grid and information may be pivoted to aggregate and disaggregate data.  Additionally, standard JustEnough functionality in this view is included to allow for saved layouts, graphing metrics and filtering the key dimensions related to the product, location and time hierarchies.
