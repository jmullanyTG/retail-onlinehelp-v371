Lifespan Side Pane

While in Placeholder or Collection mode, you can open the Lifespan side pane by selecting it from the View Options toolbar menu or by right-clicking on a column header and choosing Lifespan.

The Lifespan pane shows the entire life cycle of each of your items over time, including phase-in, phase-out, markdown periods, and any non-selling periods.

  • Blue squares indicate the planned selling period for an item.
  • Purple squares indicate the markdown period for an item.
  • Light gray squares indicate a partial non-selling period.
  • Dark gray squares indicate a non-selling period.

In the pane, you can perform the following actions:

  • Lengthen or shorten an item lifespan by clicking on its phase-in or phase-out period and dragging it to the desired period .
    • To lengthen or shorten multiple lifespan bars at once, press CTRL+ click on the selected lifespan bars, then drag them to the desired period
  • Move an entire lifespan earlier or later by right-clicking it and dragging it to the desired period.
    • To move multiple lifespan bars at once, press CTRL+ right-click on the selected lifespan bars, then drag them to the desired period.

Note: Be sure to save any changes you make.
