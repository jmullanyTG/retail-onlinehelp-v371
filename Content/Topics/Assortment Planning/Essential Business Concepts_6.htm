Essential Business Concepts

The assortment rollup is part of the standard Assortment Planning module and can be found in the Assortment section of the software. It contains information specific to Assortment Planning.

 

 

The Assortment Rollup contains all the foundational metrics related to the aggregation of sales, margin, receipt and inventory data up the product and location hierarchies defined in the active assortment plan.  Users have the ability to review future period plans as well as actualized historical data.   One of the key components of the Assortment Rollup is the enablement of users to review plan vs. actualized data for periods within the active assortment plan by utilizing the comparison plan functionality.
