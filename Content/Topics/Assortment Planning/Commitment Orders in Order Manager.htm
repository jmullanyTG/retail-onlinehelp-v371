Commitment Orders in Order Manager

To facilitate a centralized approach to the creation and processing of outgoing product orders from all JustEnough modules, commitment orders for receipts planned in Assortment Planning are handled through the Order Manager module, located under the Manage tab of the main JustEnough ribbon:

Within Order Manager, users can create, review, approve, and submit product orders to external systems through the integration established during implementation of the Assortment Planning software.
