Essential Business Concepts

  • Order Documents and Receipt Dates – to meet the requirements of the majority of specialty retailers, functionality for the determination of size and prepack breakdowns for orders. Due to the inherent inaccuracies associated with doing these analyses for multiple receipt dates simultaneously, order manager documents can only be created with a single receipt date each. Unlimited order lines, including different variants and store cluster orders, can be included as long as they all share a single receipt date. This constraint also facilitates the tracking of outstanding activities for orders through the Upcoming Commitments Dashboard in Assortment Planning (see Assortment Planning Dashboard – Assortment Upcoming Commitments Dashboard section above)
  • Order Workflow – Order Manager has been integrated with the JustEnough Workflow module to facilitate identification of and movement through the various stages of the ordering process, as defined by the needs of your organization. Multiple workflows, ranging from very simple expedited flows, where all stages are completed at once with a single submission of the order, to very complex, with several levels of approval at each stage and submissions to external systems after each, can be configured and used within Order Manager. The # of days out from the receipt date that each stage must be completed is configured in the Workflow module and then compared to the current date in the above referenced Upcoming Commitments Dashboard, allowing users to track their progress against established deliverables (for more information, please see the JustEnough Workflow Manager Standard Documentation)
  • Important: Workflow status is set at the order line level of order documents, however, all lines in a document must have the same status in order to advance the document status and submit the order document to external systems; this is mainly relevant in pre-pack stage, as order lines can be selected for prepacking individually or in groups, in addition to all order lines at once
  • Document Status vs Workflow Status – there are two types of status within Order Manager – document status and workflow status. Document status describes the current ownership or state of the order document as a whole. Document statuses include things like Forwarded, Authorized, and Uploaded, and cannot be changed without customization. Workflow status, on the other hand, describes the stage of the active workflow that the order document is currently in. Workflow statuses are configurable through the Workflow module and can include anything your organization deems important. Available workflow statuses are dependent on the workflow currently assigned to an order document
