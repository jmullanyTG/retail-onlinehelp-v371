Apply Rate of Sale Section

In this section, the user applies the results of a Rate of Sale scenario to the placeholders/collections selected in the Main Planning view.  Upon applying the results, the sales plans for the selected placeholders/collections will be adjusted to reflect the ROS values that have been applied.

  1. This section should already populate based on the selected lines in the Main Planning view, but click Execute, if needed, to update the view
  2. In the Scenario results section, check the boxes corresponding to the rates of sale you wish to apply to the selected placeholder/variant/cluster combinations
  3. Check the grand total box of the variants/placeholders or collections in which to make them eligible for the ROS values selected in the scenario results view
  4. Click Copy Rate of Sale at the top of the Apply Rate of Sale section to populate the selected ROS values from the results section
  5. Click Apply to commit the chosen rates of sale to the selected placeholders/collections as shown in the Apply Rate of Sale section

Note: Before clicking apply, users can clear any copied ROS values by clicking Undo in the top of the apply rate of sale view.
