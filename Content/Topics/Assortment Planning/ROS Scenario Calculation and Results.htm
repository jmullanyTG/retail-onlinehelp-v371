ROS Scenario Calculation and Results

Once the inputs for a scenario are set, calculation gathers the data returned by those inputs and then applies a data cleansing process to remove data points that would skew the scenario results. The cleansing process used is identical to that employed during Rationalization. For details on this process, refer to Target Creation > Rationalization within this manual, or, for an expanded explanation with sample data, see the Rate of Sale Calculator Standard Documentation.

Note:  the system will also calculate and display a raw ROS, which has not undergone data cleansing, to the user, primarily for comparison purposes, although the raw ROS can also be applied if desired

Once scenarios have been calculated, users can review the resulting rates of sale by defined performance bands (set during configuration) as both raw and adjusted (cleansed) values.  The results are grouped into the following performance percentiles (configurable during implementation):

  • Minimum
  • 25th percentile
  • Median (50th percentile)
  • 75th percentile
  • Maximum
  • Average (default selection)
  1. Note: Average shows the true average rate of sale per variant/period for the selected data, not the average from the configured percentile bands
