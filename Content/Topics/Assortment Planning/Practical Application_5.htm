Practical Application

Once the values have populated upon hitting execute, users can use this report to slice and dice their assortment for a defined timeframe.  This view can be utilized to pass key information to users that do not have access to the system.  For example, merchandise planners can export this report to a PDF or excel to share with merchants or buyers.  Additionally, this report is intended to provide insight to how an assortment is planned and how items fit into the assortment in terms of their selling behavior.  For example, by using a product attribute filter, a denim planner could look at how skinny jeans that have a certain wash are planned and how much presence in the assortment these attributes will have as they are merchandised across the selling channels of the organization.
