Assortment Planning Overview

The JustEnough Assortment Planning module provides users with an innovative perpetual approach to the assortment planning process. Leverage scenario modeling capabilities through the Target Creation process to quickly lay a sound foundation for your assortments or forge an assortment from the ground up using manual placeholder creation. Once your plan framework is created, fine tune at multiple levels, and apply supplemental analyses from integrated supporting modules to craft your ideal buy strategy. Reconcile against financial targets and other goals through a variety of reporting tools, manage order commitments through integration with Order Manager and external systems, and ultimately, track your plan accuracy and make adjustments in-season.
