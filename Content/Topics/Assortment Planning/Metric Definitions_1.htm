Metric Definitions

After the user has set the parameter selections at the top of the screen, clicking Execute will return the following metrics in the view for comparison of the two plans the user has selected.  The metrics defined below are the foundational metrics that are common between Assortment Planning and Merchandise Financial Planning:

  • Total Sales – sales revenue aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Total Sales Variance – the difference in Total Sales between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • Total Sales Variance % – the percentage above or below total sales of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • Total Units – sales units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Total Units Variance – the difference in Total Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • Total Units Variance % – the percentage above or below total units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • COGS – Cost of Sales aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • COGS Variance – the difference in COGS between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report
  • COGS Variance % – the percentage above or below COGS of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • AUR – Average Unit Retail aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Calculation: (Total Sales/Total Units)
  • AUR Variance – the difference in AUR between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report
  • AUR Variance % – the percentage above or below AUR of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • AUC – Average Unit Cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Calculation: (COGS/Total Units)
  • AUC Variance – the difference in AUC between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report
  • AUC Variance % – the percentage above or below AUC of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • AUT – Average Unit Ticket aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Calculation: (Reg. Price Sales/Reg. Price Units)
  • AUT Variance – the difference in AUT between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report.
  • AUT Variance % – the percentage above or below AUT of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • Margin – profit margin expressed in currency value aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Calculation: (Total Sales – COGS)
  • Margin Variance – the difference in Margin between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • Margin Variance % – the percentage above or below Margin of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • BOP – Beginning of Period inventory at cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • BOP Variance – the difference in BOP at cost between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • BOP Variance % -- the percentage above or below BOP of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • BOP Units – Beginning of Period inventory units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and merchandise financial planning
  • BOP Units Variance – the difference in BOP Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • BOP Units Variance % -- the percentage above or below BOP Units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • EOP – End of Period inventory at cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • EOP Variance – the difference in EOP at cost between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • EOP Variance % -- the percentage above or below EOP of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • EOP Units – End of Period inventory units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • EOP Units Variance – the difference in EOP Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • EOP Units Variance % – the percentage above or below EOP Units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • Receipts – total intake or receipts at cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Receipts Variance – the difference in Receipts at cost between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • Receipts Variance % -- the percentage above or below Receipts at cost of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
  • Receipt Units – total intake units or receipt units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
  • Receipt Units Variance – the difference in Receipt Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
  • Receipt Units Variance % – the percentage above or below Receipt Units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
