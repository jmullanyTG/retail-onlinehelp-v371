Reconciliation

Assortment plans typically contain granular data related to the item plans of an organization, as it represents the detail behind what items are being bought, when items deliver, and the lifecycle information for items related to Sales, Cost of Sales, Margin, Receipts, and Inventory ownership.  This rich information can be leveraged within an organization to view plans at various aggregated levels of the product, site and time hierarchies.  The aggregated information can be compared to financial targets set within Merchandise Financial Planning or assortment plan snapshots.

The aggregation of assortment plan data at established levels of the product and site hierarchies over time provides insight to an organization and helps to guide the organization in decisions around merchandising, pricing and promotions, open to buy, profitability and inventory management.  The reconciliation screen has been designed as a standard feature within the Assortment Planning Module in order for organizations to aggregate their assortment plan data and reconcile key metrics that are set as targets within Merchandise Financial Planning.
