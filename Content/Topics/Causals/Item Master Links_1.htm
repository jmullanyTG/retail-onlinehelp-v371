Item Master Links

The item master link will link the causal to an item master and apply it across all sites.

Selected items from the filter may be removed from the causal link by highlighting them and pressing the “delete” button. For more information on a specific item, it may be highlighted and “view” may be clicked, which will launch an Item Manager view.

If the items are not appearing in the grid, you have selected items with a mismatched bucket type to that of the causal.
