Filter Links

Selecting a filter link will link all items contained within a filter to the specific causal. This especially helpful with dynamic filters, as the items contained within the filter can change over time.

Users have the option to choose the entity type to which they wish to establish a causal filter link.

Inventory, item master, site, and variant entity filters may be linked with the actual causal link applying at the constituent inventory level.

Selected items from the filter may be removed from the causal link by highlighting them and pressing the “delete” button. For more information on a specific item, it may be highlighted and “view” may be clicked, which will launch an Item Manager view.

If the items are not appearing in the grid, items with a mismatched bucket type to that of the causal may have been selected.
