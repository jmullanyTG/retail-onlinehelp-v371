Adding or Deleting a Causal

The green “Add” button will open up the “add causal” window. Here, a user may name their causal, select the type, the bucket type, and assign a default value. The bucket type selected may be daily, weekly, or monthly, however, it is important that the bucket type selected match the forecasts bucket type.

Deleting a causal may be done by selecting it from the drop down, then clicking the “delete” button. This will eliminate any and all links.
