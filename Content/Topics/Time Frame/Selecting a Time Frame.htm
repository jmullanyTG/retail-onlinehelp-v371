Selecting a Time Frame

In modules which use time frames, you can choose a time frame from the Time Frame dialog box.

  1. Click the time frame field to edit it, then click '...' to open the Time Frames selector.

  2. In the Time Frames selector, choose the time frame you want to use.

  3. If none of the available time frames is suitable, click ( Maintain Time Frames) to open the Time Frame Maintenance window. From there, you can create a new time frame or edit an existing one to suit your purpose.
